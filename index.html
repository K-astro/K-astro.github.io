Current Research

Charting CVs in the eROSITA Sky

Developing a machine learning-enhanced Bayesian framework for:

  • Robust identification of CVs in all-sky X-ray surveys
  • Cross-matching with multi-wavelength datasets
  • Population analysis using Gaia constraints

This work enables volume-limited studies critical for understanding CV evolution and their contribution to Galactic X-ray emission.
